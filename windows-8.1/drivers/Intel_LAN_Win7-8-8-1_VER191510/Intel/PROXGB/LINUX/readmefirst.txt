This release includes the ixgbe Linux* Base Driver for Intel® 10 Gigabit 
Network Connections. 

 - ixgbe driver supports all 82599, 82598EB and X540-based 10 Gigabit Network 
   Connections.
 - ixgbevf driver supports 82599 and X540-based virtual function devices that 
   can only be activated on kernels that support SR-IOV.
